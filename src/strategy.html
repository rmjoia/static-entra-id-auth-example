Strategic pillars

Platform consolidation

Merge the three product lines into a single workspace with shared identity, billing, and data. Unified workspace launches Q3 2026.

Vertical depth

Double down on the two verticals where we have measurable NRR advantage; deprioritize the long tail.

Operational leverage

Drive gross margin from 74% to 80% via infrastructure consolidation and tier-2 support automation.

Key bets

  • Launch unified workspace by Q3 2026
  • Build or acquire a connector marketplace
  • Establish a partner channel contributing 25% of new ARR by 2028
  • Reach 80% gross margin via infra consolidation

What we are not doing

  • No Fortune-500 enterprise pursuit this period.
  • No mobile-first product line.
  • No expansion outside our two priority verticals.
  • No hardware play; cloud-only remains the platform bet.
