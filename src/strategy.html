Strategy 2026–2028

Three-year north star

Be the default measurement platform for mid-market companies in our focus verticals, reaching $150M ARR by end of 2028.

Strategic pillars

  1. Platform consolidation. Merge our three product lines into a single workspace experience with shared identity, billing, and data.
  2. Vertical depth. Double down on the two verticals where we have measurable NRR advantage; deprioritize the long tail.
  3. Operational leverage. Drive gross margin from 74% to 80% via infrastructure consolidation and tier-2 support automation.

Key bets

  • Launch the unified workspace in Q3 2026.
  • Acquire or build a connector marketplace.
  • Establish a partner channel that contributes 25% of new ARR by 2028.

What we are not doing

We are explicitly not pursuing enterprise (Fortune 500) deals in this period, and we are not building a mobile-first product line.
